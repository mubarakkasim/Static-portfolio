My name is Mubarak Kasim Maishanu, and I am a web application developer, software engineer, teacher, and data analyst based in Zaria, Kaduna State. I specialize in building scalable and efficient web applications using PHP, with strong expertise in both the Laravel and Yii2 frameworks. I work extensively with MySQL for database management and sometimes use TypeScript and Supabase when building modern, real-time, and serverless solutions.

Professionally, I work as a Software Engineer at BuyDigital Services (buydigital.ng), where I develop and maintain digital platforms that support business operations. Alongside my technical career, I serve as a Mathematics and Data Processing teacher at Al-Ahyaz Academy, where I combine industry knowledge with classroom teaching to help students understand technology and analytical thinking.

I have practical experience designing and implementing business and payment processing management systems, including E-commerce solutions, Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, and payment gateway integrations into existing business software. My focus is on improving business automation, securing financial transactions, and building structured systems that improve operational efficiency and reliability.

I approach software development with a clear vision of solving real-world problems through well-structured and scalable systems. I have a strong understanding of complex software architecture, system integration, and business-driven solution development. I am committed to building reliable, maintainable, and growth-oriented applications while working effectively with both technical teams and business stakeholders.

I remain open to collaborations, innovative projects, and professional opportunities where I can contribute my combined experience in software engineering, data analysis, and education to deliver practical and modern business technology solutions.
