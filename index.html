About Me

My name is Mubarak Kasim Maishanu, I am a web application developer with strong expertise in building dynamic, data-driven systems using modern technologies such as PHP (Laravel Framework), MySQL, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. My core focus is creating scalable web solutions that streamline operations, enhance user experience, and deliver real business value.


My current major project, Smart School Workflow System, is an integrated academic and administrative platform that automates school operations from student enrollment and staff management to report generation and result computation. The system is designed to enhance transparency, reduce manual workload, and improve real-time decision-making for educational institutions.


Beyond development, I also serve as an ICT instructor, teaching Computer Science and Data Processing, where I share practical industry knowledge and inspire students to embrace technology-driven problem-solving.


I value technical precision, collaboration, and open-source innovation, constantly seeking new opportunities to build impactful software that solves real-world problems.
